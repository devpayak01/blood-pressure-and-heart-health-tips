Daily Tips

Small, consistent actions add up. Try these evidence-based habits to steadily lower your blood pressure.

🥛

Limit Sodium

Aim for less than 1,500–2,300 mg/day. Check labels and choose fresh foods.

🚶

Move Daily

150 minutes/week of moderate activity lowers BP and boosts heart health.

🥗

Eat DASH Style

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, low-fat dairy.

🧘

Manage Stress

Try breathing exercises, mindfulness, or light yoga 10 minutes daily.

🛌

Prioritize Sleep

7–9 hours nightly helps regulate hormones and supports heart health.

💧

Hydrate

Water supports circulation and may help maintain healthy BP.
